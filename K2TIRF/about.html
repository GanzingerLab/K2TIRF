The microscope was designed specifically to become the main tool for all surface-based fluorescence imaging in the group Physics of Cellular Interactions of Dr. Ganzinger at AMOLF.
Developing and setting up the microscope was strongly supported by AMOLF's in-house engineering teams and the precision manufacturing workshop.
